## Mimic Reborn v3.1.0 — Champ select, perfected

Big quality-of-life release for champion select, pairing, and staying in the loop while queued.

### 🚀 What's new

- **Pick intent & bans fixed**: declare your pick during "Declare your intent", ban reliably, and the champion grid never comes up empty. A champion hovered earlier can be locked in directly when you reopen the picker.
- **Champion picker upgrades**: filter by role (Top/Jungle/Mid/ADC/Support, powered by the client's own data), **favorite champions** (star them, they sort first), and search that always works.
- **Pick trades**: request a champion trade with a teammate, accept or decline incoming ones.
- **Chromas & ward skins**: pick owned chromas under each skin, and change your ward skin (applied to your account loadout, like the desktop client).
- **Rune pages**: a page is auto-created when your account has none, plus create/rename/delete from the phone.
- **Role & spell swapping**: picking a role or summoner spell already in the other slot now swaps the two.
- **Real names**: teammates show their Riot ID names everywhere instead of "Summoner".
- **Easier pairing**: scan the QR code from inside the app (no camera permission needed) or let the app **find your PC automatically** on your network — typing an IP is now the last resort.
- **Update from your PC**: Conduit caches the latest released APK and the app offers a one-tap update over LAN whenever a newer version is available.
- **Chat**: talk with your lobby and champ select from the phone — a discreet floating button with an unread badge.
- **Queue status notification**: persistent silent notification with elapsed/estimated time while queued.
- **Stays awake, stays connected**: the screen stays on while connected, brief Wi-Fi drops no longer reset the UI, and the phone buzzes when it's your turn to pick or ban.
- **Proper app icon** and splash screens on Android.

### 📦 Installation

1. **PC**: download `MimicConduit.exe` below and run it (no .NET install needed). It sits in the system tray.
2. **Phone**: either scan the QR code from the tray icon in your browser (same Wi-Fi), or install `Mimic.apk` (Android) — the app finds your PC automatically.
3. Approve the device on your PC — once per device. Done!

Away from home? Use [Tailscale](https://tailscale.com/) or any VPN to your home network.

### ⚖️ Riot policy

Mimic Reborn only uses the official local League Client API (LCU) — like Blitz or Porofessor. No memory reading, no injection, no gameplay automation: it only relays actions you take manually. Use at your own risk, as with any third-party tool.
